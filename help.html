Asteroids is a two-dimensional space shooter game. You score points by destroying the asteroids. The aim of the game is to get the highest score.

You control your spaceship to avoid and eliminate the endless supply of asteroids that threaten to destroy it. You will start with 3 lives in a game and you will lose a life every time you crash into a asteroid. Once you destroy all the asteroids on the screen a new wave will begin with even more asteroids.

When you start the game, your spaceship will be in the middle of the screen. Heading toward your ship will be 3 large asteroids.Your job is to blast those rocks. However, when you blast them, they break up into 2 medium rocks. Blasting a medium rock gives you 2 small rocks. These different sized rocks all have varied points for destroying them.

POINTS:
Object Points
Large Asteroids10 points
Medium Asteroids50 points
Small Asteroids100 points

In order to become a master of this game, you must be familiar with the buttons that control the ship. These are:

  • Left Rotation Button
  • Right Rotation Button
  • Fire Button
  • Thrust Button
  • Shield Button

CONTROL BUTTONS:

  • To rotate right you press the right arrow key.
  • To rotate left you press the left arrow key.
  • To thrust the ship you press the forward arrow key. The more you hold it down the faster the ship will go but look out for those Asteroids.
  • To fire bullets from the ship you can press or hold down the spacebar on the keyboard.
  • To activate the shield around the ship you press the 's' key on the keyboard.

SHOOTING BULLETS:

Keep in mind a couple of things when shooting:

  • You can have 4 bullets on the screen at any one time. This is useful for when you are blasting rocks at close range.
  • Your bullets "wrap around" the screen. This means any bullet that goes past the edge of the screen will reappear on the opposite side traveling the same direction.

SHIELD:

  • When the game is started and when a next wave has began, the ship will have an activated shield around it to protect it from the asteroid.
  • This shield will be on the ship for 10 seconds, so quickly get away from those asteroids.
  • With this shield you will be able to hit a Asteroid and not lose a life.
  • This shield will be available to you when the shield box on the bottom left of the screen turns yellow.
